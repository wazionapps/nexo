Important nuance

The benchmark is narrower than the product

LoCoMo measures long-horizon memory quality. The current NEXO runtime also includes startup preflight, doctor diagnostics, recovery-aware jobs, personal scripts, and update/runtime operations that are outside the benchmark scope.

Benchmark is the hook. Runtime behavior is the moat.

Use the benchmark as evidence of retrieval quality, then evaluate the runtime itself with install, doctor, and wake recovery.
