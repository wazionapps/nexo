    Positioning

    What teams buy is not just AI replies. It is operator continuity.

    Conversation surface

    NEXO Desktop gives teams one place to see the live queue, the active thread, the draft response, and the next action without jumping between tabs and hidden notes.

    Context surface

    The right-hand panel is not decoration. It turns customer memory, sentiment, tags, ownership, and tasks into something operators can use immediately.

    Execution surface

    The point is operational flow: see what happened, answer with context, leave the task, preserve the handoff, and keep the relationship intact.

    Brain vs Desktop

    Two layers, one story

    NEXO Brain

    The open-source cognitive engine: shared brain, memory, retrieval, continuity, and runtime discipline.

    • Libre/open-source core
    • Local cognitive runtime
    • Multi-client memory and workflow foundation
    NEXO Desktop

    The commercial business application built on top of that engine for teams who need a direct operational surface.

    • Commercial packaged workspace
    • Customer-facing operations and team continuity
    • Private walkthrough and rollout path
