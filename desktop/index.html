What is actually in the product

Conversation workspace

Multiple conversations live in the left sidebar, with a dedicated active thread in the main area and a composer at the bottom.

Archived threads

Past conversations can be archived, searched, restored, or deleted from the archived section without cluttering the main list.

Search in two layers

There is in-conversation search for the active thread and quick search across conversations for reopening the right context fast.

Tool cards and streaming output

NEXO replies in streaming mode and tool activity is surfaced as readable cards instead of raw terminal noise.

Operator controls

The top bar exposes search, export to Markdown, and stop controls while the composer keeps status, attachments, and reply state visible.

Onboarding and preferences

The app includes onboarding, profile and personality tabs, a dedicated NEXO Desktop preferences tab, and advanced/system views.
