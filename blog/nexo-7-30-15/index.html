NEXO 7.30.15: Email NEXO lifecycle closure

June 5, 2026. This patch fixes the reply lifecycle path used by Email NEXO and its background monitor.

What changed

  • nexo-send-reply.py can force the correct reply lifecycle event with --classify-as.
  • Email monitor prompts now tell agents to send real closures as --classify-as resolution, even when the reply starts with a short affirmation.
  • Debt scans now respect newer resolution / action_done events before re-emitting stale acknowledgement or commitment debt.
  • Crash-after-send recovery reconciles stale processing rows with sent reply events back to processed instead of reopening them.
  • Five regression tests pin the affirmative-instruction closure and no-reopen behavior.

Why it matters

Operator replies often start naturally with "yes", "perfect", or "agreed" and then include real instructions. 7.30.15 lets the email agent record the actual lifecycle outcome at send time and prevents the monitor from waking old work that was already closed.
