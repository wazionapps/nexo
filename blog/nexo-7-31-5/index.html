NEXO 7.31.5: the breaker resume notice

June 11, 2026. A small patch that keeps a promise.

What changed

  • The resume notice exists now. When automations pause because the selected engine is unavailable (credits, rate limits, expired session), the single pause notice says "you will get another notice when work resumes". 7.31.5 delivers exactly that: one "engine resumed, the queue is processing" email per recovery, in the operator's language, signed by their agent — never repeated.

Found while reviewing the circuit breaker's first real production firing, which otherwise behaved exactly as designed: paused, queued, one notice.
