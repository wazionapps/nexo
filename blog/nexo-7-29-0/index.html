NEXO 7.29.0 - morning briefing preferences

v7.29.0 is a Brain minor release focused on making the daily morning briefing useful for non-technical Desktop users. The morning agent now accepts structured content preferences, keeps forced operational safety sections protected, and stores the latest sent briefing for Desktop Home and overlay views.

The release adds a presentation contract for email output: subject, plain-text body, and optional sanitized HTML. That keeps the briefing readable in email while preserving safe plain-text fallbacks for Desktop and automation surfaces.

Email accounts also gain per-account signatures. Brain stores the signature, exposes a short preview for Desktop, and appends it safely to outgoing replies and briefings for that account.

The release includes schema 77 coverage for fresh installs and updates, plus contract tests for briefing lookup, preference persistence, signature handling, and sanitized email presentation.
