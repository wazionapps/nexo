NEXO 7.31.10 - Local Memory downranks boilerplate email noise

v7.31.10 is a patch release over v7.31.9. It improves Local Memory search quality when a query matches both a strong document and a low-signal email.

The scorer now applies bounded, deterministic quality tie-breaks. Contracts, invoices, payroll and quotes keep priority, while newsletters, CSS-heavy email bodies, legal footers and low-signal account notices lose ties unless they contain a strong document signal.

This keeps local evidence retrieval closer to the user's intent: a search for a contract, invoice or payroll document should not be displaced by an unrelated email template that happened to contain overlapping names or company words.
