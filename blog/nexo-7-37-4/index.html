NEXO 7.37.4 - product-gap reporting and briefing hygiene

v7.37.4 is a product-quality patch over v7.37.3. It closes loops where internal automation kept creating operator noise after the underlying product gap was already known or covered.

Deep Sleep product gaps now become sanitized tickets. Recurring patterns that call for product work are reported to NEXO Desktop support with stable dedupe and redaction for paths, URLs, emails, bootstrap filenames, and secret-looking values.

Self-audit and morning briefing now respect resolved state. The daily self-audit preserves closed internal opportunities instead of reopening them, and the morning briefing reads item history before presenting a topic as a fresh decision.

Support ticket tooling now matches the live backend. Brain can list, read, create, add evidence to, close, and reopen real support tickets through the backend contract.
