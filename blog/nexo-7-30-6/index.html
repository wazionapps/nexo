NEXO 7.30.6 - Deep Sleep retention

v7.30.6 is a focused Brain patch over v7.30.5. It bounds Deep Sleep operational storage so overnight analysis does not keep accumulating database backups, context dumps, and oversized sleep logs forever.

The new retention policy keeps the three newest backup-nexo.db files, the three newest backup-cognitive.db files, and the seven newest analyzed context dumps. Unfinished or unanalyzed contexts are preserved so a failed Deep Sleep run can still be retried or debugged.

Existing installations clean themselves during update through the Deep Sleep heal path. New installations enforce the same policy at the start and end of nightly Deep Sleep, with daily sleep housekeeping as an additional safety net.

Oversized Deep Sleep logs are truncated in place to a recent tail instead of archived into another disk consumer. This release deliberately does not touch local-context memory; it only fixes Deep Sleep/log retention.
