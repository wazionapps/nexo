NEXO 7.30.10: packaged update repair baseline

June 4, 2026. This patch closes the packaged-update gap found during local verification of 7.30.9.

What changed

  • npm-installed `nexo update` now writes `operations/last-repair-baseline.json` after successful import verification.
  • Same-version idempotent maintenance also stamps the baseline, so interrupted or already-updated installs can self-heal without waiting for another version bump.
  • Tests now cover the packaged path, the runtime post-sync path, and ordering after import verification.

Why it matters

The 7.30.9 doctor/gate fix was correct, but npm-installed users also needed the baseline written by their normal update path. 7.30.10 makes that automatic.
