NEXO 7.23.10 - operational copy alignment

v7.23.10 is a Brain operational-copy patch. The open-source Brain CLI/update path keeps its runtime output in English, while NEXO Desktop owns Spanish/English localization for Desktop users.

This keeps the public Brain package language contract clean and avoids leaking Desktop-specific localized copy into the open-source runtime.
