NEXO 7.30.19: product knowledge hub

June 6, 2026. This patch turns product capability knowledge into a structured, validated catalog that agents can query before answering what NEXO can do.

What changed

  • src/product_knowledge/ now stores source-linked capabilities with safety, consent, retention, live-state and answer-guidance metadata.
  • Brain exposes read-only product tools for capability search, explanation, product answers, surface status and catalog validation.
  • nexo_system_catalog still resolves legacy capability names such as nexo_provider_models, now backed by the structured catalog.
  • Release tests validate the catalog and tool map so product self-knowledge cannot drift silently.

Why it matters

NEXO can now explain its product capabilities from a contract, not from stale prompt prose. Live facts such as credits balances, provider models, support tickets and cloud resources remain delegated to backend/runtime checks.
